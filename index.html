Who We Are

SPEED, a non-profit organization committed to revolutionizing waste management for a sustainable future. At SPEED, we believe in the power of innovation and community-driven initiatives to tackle the pressing challenges posed by waste. Through collaborative efforts, education, and technology, we strive to implement effective waste management strategies that not only reduce environmental impact but also create a cleaner, healthier world for generations to come. Join us in our mission to transform waste into opportunities and pave the way towards a greener tomorrow. Moreover, we are into financial literacy, livelihood, tree plantation, awreness on wetland conservation, health etc of the local people of the Sualkuchi village so far.

Vision

Protect Environment and promote Livelihood.

Mission

Targeting the Environment by way of Solid Waste management, plantation, wetland protection and livelihood of the marginalized segment.

Goal

To have positive impact on 500000 people in waste management and livelihood creation by 2030.
